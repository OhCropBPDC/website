About OhCrop!

OhCrop is BPDC's premier graphic design club, where creativity meets technology. We strive to bring together students who are passionate about graphic design and provide them with the resources and opportunities to hone their skills.

Our mission is to empower students to express themselves through visual storytelling and design. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned designer, OhCrop is a community where you can grow, collaborate, and innovate.

What We Do

  • Workshops: We regularly host workshops on tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma to help students master the basics and advanced techniques in graphic design.
  • Projects: We take on exciting design projects for campus events, collaborating with other clubs and departments to showcase the best designs.
  • Competitions: We encourage members to participate in local and national design competitions, giving them the chance to showcase their talent on a broader stage.
